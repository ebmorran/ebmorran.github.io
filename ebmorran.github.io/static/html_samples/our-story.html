Hey there! Thank you again for signing up to get news and deals from Orchard. 🙂 We wanted to take a moment to tell you a bit about Orchard and our mission: giving Canadians mobile freedom. Here's why that mission matters.

Let's start with one fact you may already know: Canadians pay the most in the world for mobile. By a lot.

On top of the exorbitant prices we pay for mobile service, many of us are stuck in our carrier's two-year device financing cycle, locking us in to expensive contracts. Bringing your own phone lets you take back power from the carriers — but with the latest iPhones running over $1000, is that power something the average Canadian can afford?

Used devices make mobile freedom affordable. But buying a used phone on the Internet can sometimes feel like the Wild West. (In fact, we recently partnered with CBC's Marketplace to uncover some of the risks of buying used phones online.)

That's why Orchard exists: to make it easier and safer for you to get a premium device, while keeping money in your pocket. What sets us apart?

  • Obsessive quality testing. We test each phone so thoroughly, you'd think it came straight from Apple or Samsung.
  • 5-star customer support. Before and after you buy, our tech-savvy Customer Advocates are here to answer all your questions. They're nerds who speak human, so there's no need to know everything about smartphones just to buy and use one. 🙂
  • 90-day, no-questions-asked return policy. Change your mind or run into issues? We'll refund, repair or replace the phone at no cost to you.

Orchard has been around since 2014, and in that time we've become smartphone experts. As a member of our email list, you can look forward to benefiting from that expertise.

Keep an eye out for our next update, where we'll take a look at one of the big questions of buying a smartphone: used or new?

See you soon!
—The Orchard Team
