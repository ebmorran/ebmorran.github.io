Elizabeth Morran

Writing the “why.”

My aim as a writer is to integrate purpose and insight, illuminating the “what” for the sake of the “why.” With a keen eye for clarity and structure, I develop effective support content, persuasive email marketing copy and in-depth, SEO-tuned blog posts.

As a Customer Service Team Lead, I have three years of experience manifesting a consistent voice through concise, helpful content — in dozens of customer emails and chat interactions per day.
